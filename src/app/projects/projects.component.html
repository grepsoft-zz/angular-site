Content Management System
OpticalThirty8

Optical38 wanted to move away form paper based invoicing and order intake to digital one.They wanted to be able to track orders and send text message notification to customes when their order was ready and also reminding them about appointments.
We took their paper based invoice system and converted it into web based system. It enables their employees to walk around the store with their iPads and input orders. Through the web based system they are also able to schedule appointments and maintain inventory.

Pickup and Delivery
ABCDev

ABCDev has clients in the transportation industry and they wanted an app that can be used for pickup and delivery. They wanted the ability for drivers to log in and out, scan barcodes, view assignments, collect signatures, complete tasks, change freight type all on a mobile device.

Location-Based Service
ABCDev

ABCDev has clients in the transportation industry and they wanted an app that can be used for proof of delivery running on Android.
We wrote an Android app running on the Zebra TC-56 hardware that enables a driver to take picture. The app also burns the timestamp and gps coordinates on the image and send it to a server. The client can then see the image(s) later on and make sure the delivery is made to the right place in case of a dispute.
